# RISHIKESH-RIVER-RAFTING-DASHBOARD
This dashboard shows the overall performance of the Rishikesh River Rafting business. It includes important details like total customers (8,960), total revenue (14M), total profit (5M), booking cancellations, and customer churn.

The dashboard explains how business changes month by month. It shows price differences in peak, moderate, and off-season months and also displays weather risk levels to understand how weather affects bookings. Revenue and profit are higher in peak months and lower in off-season months.

It also shows customer churn and cancelled bookings by month, which helps identify months where customers leave or cancel more. A map is used to show from which regions customers are coming, including domestic and international locations. The average rating by month helps understand customer satisfaction.

this dashboard helps in better planning, pricing decisions, and improving customer experience.
